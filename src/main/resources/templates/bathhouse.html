TAR KATAL: Liquid Luck

Instructions: Figure out the correct combination of SPECIAL INGREDIENTS to turn the potion to its required color. A color will always have a unique reaction output color when mixed with a specific ingredient. However, you may only add a certain number of ingredient (different per level) to complete the transformation before it turns into SLUDGE (meaning you need to start all over again).

There are THREE levels to this task, and each level has completely different potion reaction rules.
